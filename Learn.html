Learn


Being a part of the DVNSW member community allows organisations and individuals to join us in raising awareness about gender inequality and being part of a strong collective voice where women, children, families and communities live free from violence.

As part of your membership you will receive the following:


  1. Up to date sector news, information and events Advocacy, and professional networking opportunities

  2. Access to a Peak Body who can advocate to Government or other relevant institutions or professionals on your behalf

  3. Opportunities to participate in Communities of Practice, forums and other sector related events

  4. Discounted tickets to the DVNSW Biannual Conference

  5. Access to free Christmas hampers for women and children at Christmas and donated goods throughout the year
